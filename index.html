FAQ

Questions before you load the bar?

What is a barbell plate calculator?

A barbell plate calculator converts a target weight into the plates needed on each side of the bar. RackMath includes the bar weight so the total load is easy to verify.

Does RackMath work for pounds and kilograms?

Yes. RackMath is designed for both pound and kilogram plate math, including common gym plates and competition-style loading.

Can I use RackMath without installing an app?

Yes. The web version runs in your browser, which makes it easy to use on a phone near the rack or on a laptop while programming training.
