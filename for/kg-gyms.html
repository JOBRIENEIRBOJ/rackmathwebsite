Switch to kilogram loading, account for kg bars and plates, and avoid pound-based assumptions when training or coaching in metric gyms.

Use case

What RackMath solves here.

Metric gyms should not have to work around pound-based assumptions. Bar weights, plate jumps, and program conversions all need to make sense in kilograms.

RackMath supports kg-first plate loading and conversion workflows so metric lifters can train without translating every set by hand.
