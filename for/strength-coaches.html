Use case

What RackMath solves here.

Coaching gets harder when the plan lives in one place and the athlete executes it somewhere else. Plate math, substitutions, and missed context can slow the session down.

RackMath helps turn written programming into trackable sessions athletes can run with clearer loads, warmups, and progress context.
