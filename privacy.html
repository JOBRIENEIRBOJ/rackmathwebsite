Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Overview

RackMath provides barbell plate calculation, workout session, workout history, and progress tracking tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information RackMath may collect, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

RackMath may collect account information such as email address, display name, authentication provider, and account status when you create or use an account.

RackMath may collect workout and app data you enter or generate in the app, including exercises, sets, reps, weights, workout history, personal records, plate settings, equipment settings, notes, goals, and related preferences.

RackMath may collect subscription and billing status information needed to provide paid features, such as plan type, subscription status, expiration dates, billing provider, and purchase identifiers. Payment card details are handled by payment processors or app stores and are not stored by RackMath.

RackMath may collect technical information such as device type, browser type, app version, diagnostic logs, and basic usage events to maintain, secure, and improve the app.

How We Use Information

RackMath uses information to provide the app, sync account data, save workout history, calculate and display progress, manage subscriptions, troubleshoot issues, prevent abuse, and improve the product.

RackMath does not sell personal information.

Service Providers

RackMath may use third-party service providers for hosting, authentication, data storage, analytics, payment processing, subscription management, email, and app infrastructure. These providers process information only as needed to support RackMath services.

Data Retention

RackMath keeps account and workout information for as long as needed to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records. Users may request account deletion where supported by the app or by contacting RackMath.

Your Choices

You can choose what workout information you enter into RackMath. You may also update account information, manage subscription access through the applicable billing provider, or request deletion of your account data.

Children

RackMath is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact RackMath so the information can be reviewed and deleted where appropriate.

Changes

RackMath may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact RackMath through the app or the public RackMath website.
