Features

Weight lifting calculator features for the moments between sets.

RackMath keeps the interface calm and the calculations precise, so loading a bar and tracking the workout never become the hardest part of training.

Bar loading

Exact plates per side.

Type a target weight and instantly see the plate combination for each side of the bar. RackMath accounts for the bar weight first, then builds the cleanest loading pattern from the plates available.

Units

Built for lb and kg gyms.

Switch unit systems for commercial gyms, Olympic weightlifting clubs, powerlifting meets, garage gyms, and hybrid facilities that use both pound and kilogram plates.

Custom setups

Match your actual rack.

Use common plates out of the box or tailor the calculator around your plate inventory, specialty bars, collars, and preferred loading style.

Warmups

Plan jumps without breaking rhythm.

RackMath helps you move from warmup to working weight with clear jumps. It is useful for solo training, meet day attempt selection, and coaches managing several lifters at once.

Web access

Fast on the device already in your hand.

The web app is responsive, quick to open, and easy to use from a phone near the rack, a tablet in the gym, or a desktop while programming training blocks.

Plate calculator

Exact plates every set

Calculate what to load and keep the answer connected to saved workouts, warmups, and history.
