## Meta Description Rules

Meta descriptions should be:

- 140–160 characters when possible
- Clear
- Useful
- Not clickbait
- Written for the searcher

Examples:

> Learn how to start your first week of weight lifting with a simple plan, manageable weights, and less gym confusion.

> New to barbell training? Learn how to load a barbell, calculate plates, and avoid common beginner plate math mistakes.

> Feeling awkward in the gym? Here is a simple guide to getting comfortable with weights, machines, racks, and your first workouts.

## Keyword Use Rules

Do not keyword-stuff.

Good:

> A barbell plate calculator helps you load the right weight without doing gym math in your head.

Bad:

> This barbell plate calculator is the best barbell plate calculator for people looking for a barbell plate calculator.

Use the main keyword naturally in:

- Title if possible
- First 100 words if natural
- One subheading if natural
- Meta description
- Slug
