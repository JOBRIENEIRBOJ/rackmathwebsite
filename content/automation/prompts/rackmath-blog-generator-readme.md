## Publishing Workflow

Use this weekly process:

1. Generate article ideas.
2. Pick one article.
3. Create a short research packet.
4. Draft the article.
5. Edit and proofread.
6. Check citations.
7. Export final Markdown.
8. Publish to RackMath.com.
9. Convert the article into social posts.

## Important Rule

The GPT should never invent citations.

If a claim needs support and no source is available, it should either:

1. Find a reliable source, or
2. Rewrite the claim more safely, or
3. Remove the claim.
